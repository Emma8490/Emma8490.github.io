Best Curly Hair Care Tips

1. Satin or Silk Pillowcases reduce Frizz

2. Maintain a Healthy Diet

3. Limit tight hairstyles

4. Only Brush with product or when wet

5. Avoid heat treatments and as Hair Dryers

6. Limit the use of drying products such as shampoos

7. Use leave in conditioners and hair masks for de-tangling

8. Use a Rosemary and Mint oil at the roots for growth, limiting frizz, and strengthening your hair
